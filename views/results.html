Exposure Data


The Exposure Data bar chart gives a quick glance, breakdown of community members who have been exposed to Covid-19, are currently positive, or are in quarantine. Currently, this is based off data from the entirety of data from the initial data collection start date.

Commuter Data Chart


The Commuter Data Chart is a pie chart that visualizes the breakdown of community members who are either a commuter, or non-commuter that have taken the Covid-19 Tracker survey. This is helpful to see for potential mitigation policies depending on the bulk of case numbers for each segment.

Academic Data Chart


The Academic Data Chart is a pie chart that visualizes the breakdown of community members who are either a current undergraduate student, graduate student, faculty, or staff member that has taken the Covid-19 Tracker survey. This is helpful to see for potential mitigation policies depending on the bulk of case numbers for each segment.
